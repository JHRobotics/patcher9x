Extra utilities (in EXTRA folder)
============================================================
CREGFIX.COM    Clear control registry.
               https://github.com/mintsuki/cregfix
SIMD95.COM     Enable AVX/SSE instructions.
               https://github.com/JHRobotics/simd95
SPLIT8MB.EXE   Make space in low 16 MB, that needs some drivers.
               From original Rloew's PATCHMEM
AHCI           Rloew's AHCI driver.
               http://lonecrusader.x10host.com/rloew/ahci9x.html
SATA           Rloew's SATA driver + patch.
               http://lonecrusader.x10host.com/rloew/ptchsata.html
INTELINF       LoneCrusader's newer Intel chipset support.
               http://lonecrusader.x10host.com/intel_inf.html
